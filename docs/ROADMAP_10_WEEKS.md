# 10-Week Journey: Building the New Travel Lounge 2026

This plan outlines how we will build and launch your new travel platforms in 5 clear steps, delivering a new part of the system every 2 weeks.

---

## Delivery 1: System Core & Basic Staff Tools (Weeks 1-2)

**Goal**: Build the "brain" of the system and start the private area for your team.

- **The Main Engine & Security**: Setting up the central system that stores all your information safely and applying your signature "Premium Red" style.
- **Staff Control Center (Part 1)**: A private login area for your team to start organizing different travel categories like Hotels, Tours, and Flights.

## Delivery 2: Complete Staff Control Center (Weeks 3-4)

**Goal**: Delivering the **Backend and Admin App together** so your team has everything needed to manage the business.

- **The Full Business Suite**: A complete set of tools for your team to add, edit, or remove all travel information (Hotels, Cruises, and Packages).
- **Photo & Price Management**: A simple way to upload beautiful travel photos and set the latest pricing.
- **Ready for Data Entry**: At the end of these 2 weeks, your team can start filling the system with all your real-world travel information.

---

## Delivery 3: Main Customer Booking Website (Weeks 5-6)

**Goal**: Launch the public website where your customers can browse and book trips.

- **The NEW Homepage**: A stunning, modern website front page that welcomes your customers and shows off your best deals.
- **Trip Search & Details**: Easy tools for customers to find the perfect hotel or tour and see all the beautiful details your staff entered.
- **Secure Bookings**: A simple way for customers to book and pay safely, with automatic tickets sent to their email.

---

## Delivery 4: Android Phone App Release (Weeks 7-8)

**Goal**: Put your travel agency directly onto your customers' Android phones.

- **Android App Launch**: An official app for Android devices, making it easy for customers to book on the go.
- **Seamless Browsing**: Customers can manage their bookings and see new offers right from their phone home screen.
- **Play Store Listing**: Focused effort to get your app live and searchable on the Google Play Store.

---

## Delivery 5: iPhone App Release & Final Handover (Weeks 9-10)

**Goal**: Complete the system with a premium iPhone app and the grand official launch.

- **iPhone App Launch**: A beautiful, high-speed app designed specifically for Apple users for a premium mobile experience.
- **App Store Approval**: We manage the official approval process with Apple to ensure the app is live in the App Store.
- **Final Handover & Launch**: A final check of every detail, handing over the "keys" to the entire system, and officially launching Travel Lounge 2026! 🚀
